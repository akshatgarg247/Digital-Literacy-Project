# Do's
<b> Think before you post </b> - consider how it may affect your reputation. <br>
<b> Protect your privacy </b> - use strong passwords and adjust privacy settings. <br>
<b> Verify information before sharing </b> - avoid spreading misinformation. <br>
<b> Be respectful and positive </b> - engage politely with others online. <br>
<b> Use social media for learning and networking </b> - follow educational pages and professionals. <br>

# Don'ts
<b> Don't share data </b> - Don't share your personal or sensitive information online. <br>
<b> Don't post inappropriate content </b> - This could harm your image. <br>
<b> Don't engange in online bullying </b> - This is considered Cyber-bullying and is a bad practice. <br>
<b> Don't believe rumours without checking </b> - This could lead to even more rumours if shared. <br>
