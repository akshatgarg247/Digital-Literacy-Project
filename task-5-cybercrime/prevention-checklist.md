# Prevention Tips
Use strong, unique passwords for every account and enable 2-factor authentication wherever possible. <br>

Keep your apps and devices updated to avoid security vulnerabilities. <br>

Verify links before clicking especially those sent via WhatsApp, email, or SMS. <br>

Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive tasks like banking <br>

# UPI & Financial Safety
Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone. <br>

Do not approve money requests from unknown users scammers often trick users into paying instead of receiving money. <br>

Double-check QR codes before scanning fake QR codes can redirect payments to fraudsters. <br>

Beware of fake links which may redirect to fake websites or steal and grab login tokens for different websites and apps. <br>

# If something goes wrong
Report immidiately on the [National Cyber Crime Portal](cybercrime.gov.in) <br>

Call Helpline: 1930 (for financial fraud & cybercrime emergencies) <br>

Act quickly, early reporting increases the chances of recovering lost money.
